Customer Support Policy and Procedures

1. Support Hours:
   - Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM EST
   - Weekend: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM EST
   - Emergency Support: 24/7 via hotline

2. Contact Methods:
   - Phone: 1-800-SUPPORT (1-800-787-7678)
   - Email: support@company.com
   - Live Chat: Available on website
   - Ticket System: support.company.com

3. Response Times:
   - Critical Issues: 2 hours
   - High Priority: 4 hours
   - Normal Issues: 24 hours
   - General Inquiries: 48 hours

4. Password Reset Process:
   - Visit password.company.com
   - Enter email address
   - Check email for reset link
   - Click link and set new password
   - Password must be 8+ characters with numbers and symbols

5. Refund Policy:
   - 30-day money-back guarantee
   - Contact support within 30 days of purchase
   - Provide order number and reason
   - Refund processed within 5-7 business days

6. Technical Support:
   - Remote desktop assistance available
   - Screen sharing for troubleshooting
   - Knowledge base articles at help.company.com
   - Video tutorials for common issues
